HOW TO MAKE A MOVE:

When the terminal / cmd ask you to make a move, type in either p or s or f or m to attack with either punch or sword or flame or magic respectively. 

HOW MUCH EACH ATTACK COSTS?

The punch attack doesn't cost any exp but sword costs 10, flame costs 20 and magic costs 50.
Note: if you attack is critical you gain 10 exp and 1 level.

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN I LOSE A GAME?

You level gets reduced by 1.

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN I WIN A GAME?

You level gets incremented by 1.

HOW TO DELETE MY SAVE?

Go to the save folder and delete all the .txt files in it.
